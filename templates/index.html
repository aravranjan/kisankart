KissanKart

Directly from the Farm to your Home

FARMER CONSUMER

KissanKart connects farmers directly with consumers, removing middlemen and ensuring fair prices. Farmers set their own rates, earn more for their hard work, and consumers get fresh produce at honest prices. A transparent, ethical marketplace built on trust.

How it Works...

1) Farmer registration: Sign up on the platform and list their available produce.

2) Platform Coordination: The platform manages visibility by ensuring farmer's product reaches the potential customer.

3) Consumer Ordering: Browse and place orders for fresh produce directly from farmers

4) Direct Payment: Receive payments directly, ensuring fair compensation for their products
